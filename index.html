We Are

NINE

We are a Czech development company and we are engaged in the construction of low-energy family houses with a garden. Our buildings are economical, ecological and meet the standards of quality and modern housing.

Your satisfaction is our priority. When preparing a project, we always meet your wishes and requirements as much as possible, including careful selection of the most suitable place for construction. During the implementation itself, we emphasize the quality of the materials used and the work performed.

Locations

We build where you want to live

All amenities are available in Cítov, which is only 8 km from Mělník and 12 km from Roudnice nad Labem. Here you will find a kindergarten and primary school, a summer swimming pool, a local public library and a municipal cultural center. Sports lovers will also enjoy themselves thanks to the area with football, volleyball and universal pitches and tennis courts. In addition, from this location there is a beautiful view of the Czech landmark, Mount Říp.
